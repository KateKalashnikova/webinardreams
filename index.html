    Code of Online Conduct

    To maintain a respectful and inclusive environment, users must follow these rules:

      No Pornographic Content.
      Sharing, posting, or linking to pornographic or sexually explicit material is strictly prohibited.

      No Hate Speech.
      Any form of hate speech, including content that attacks or demeans individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics is not tolerated.

      No Racist Remarks.
      Racist language, slurs, or behavior—whether explicit or implied—is strictly forbidden.

      No Discrimination.
      Discriminatory comments or actions toward any individual or group are not allowed. All users must treat one another with dignity and respect.

      General Conduct: Be respectful in all communications.
      Avoid harassment, threats, or any form of abusive behavior.
      Respect others' privacy and personal boundaries.

    Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent ban from accessing services. If you experience or witness violations, report them to Patrick@Webinardream.ca and include Code of Conduct Violation to the subject line. Include Date, Time, and Details.
